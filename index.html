Syntax: (x-axis Equation), (y-axis equation), (z-axis equation). Ex: (x+(sin(x-y)/10)),(y+((y+z)/10)),(z+((z+x)/10)). Z is a variable

Syntax: (x-axis Equation), (y-axis equation), (z-axis equation). Ex: (x+5),(y**2+x),(x+y-2). Z is not a variable

For Regular Equation use sqrt() for the Square root function. Sin(), cos(), and tan() are in radians. Do not use equals sign. Use x & y as variables. ** for exponetial. * for multiplication. / for division.
